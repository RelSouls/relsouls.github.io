About me

Welcome.

My name is Raúl de Miguel Martín and I am a junior videogame designer and artist, who loves 2D art and soundtracks. I currently want to focus on learning new things and improving myself so, in the future, I can make the best videogames possible.

In this page you will find a huge portion of my personal work, developed through this past years, as personal or university proyects. I have studied videogame design and development in the Rey Juan Carlos University in Madrid (Spain).

I have always been influenced by FromSoftware's games (Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Elden Ring...), for both music and art, but I have also received influence from other worlds. Now, I invite to visit my worlds, those that may hide the most unthinkable secrets.

Projects

These past years, I have developed some projects, some for university, and some for myself. In this section, I will briefly introduce them to you.

In the following sections, I will call back these projects whenever the songs or the art are related with them.

The Last Philosopher

The Last Philosopher is an ambitious project that only exists in concept arts and design documents. Inspired by Hollow Knight, this game was born like a mysterious metroidvania that settles its themes around philosophical concepts.

I would really love to bring this game to life sometime in my life, but, for the moment, this is what I have. If you want to know more about this project and its story, check the ilustrations and the video trailer figuring below.

Chess: Holy War

Chess: Holy War is a new imagination of the classic chess, with touches of modern turn-based strategy RPG. In this game, each piece has its own stats and personality. We also created two brand new pieces, that we will see below. You can play it on Itch.io by clicking here.

In this game, I made the 2D ilustration of the pieces, aswell as the music, the particles effects, and the environment art of the game.

The Friends We have

The Friends We Have was an art project which I made for the university alongside a friend. I was in charge of the images and concepts, aswell as the music for the animatic we created.

Hau (game in development)

And last, but not least, the biggest project I am currently working on. Hau is a platform videogame with metroidvania mechanics which main activity consists in collecting fruits.

Unfortunately, for the moment I can't show anything from this game but a song and a few concept arts.

Soudtracks

I have been composing soundtracks since 2015, inspired by Dark souls music and aesthetic.

Although I have never studied music specifically, I have managed to learn on my own and I have achieved very satisfying results in the recent years.

It's important to mention that not all the music is similar to Dark Souls games, as I have also tried different styles and topics. Let's check them out!

Soul Belfry Theme (Ambient)

As seen before, the Soul Belfry is a peaceful and quite place, which tries to evoque a nostalgic feeling of home and security. The player could expend hours wandering around this place, looking for a rest moment before diving into a new mission.
