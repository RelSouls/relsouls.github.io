About me

Welcome.

My name is Raúl de Miguel Martín and I am a junior videogame designer and artist, who loves 2D art and soundtracks. I currently want to focus on learning new things and improving myself so, in the future, I can make the best videogames possible.

In this page you will find a huge portion of my personal work, developed through this past years, as personal or university proyects. I have studied videogame design and development in the Rey Juan Carlos University in Madrid (Spain).

I have always been influenced by FromSoftware's games (Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Elden Ring...), for both music and art, but I have also received influence from other worlds. Now, I invite to visit my worlds, those that may hide the most unthinkable secrets.
