About projects

These past years, I have developed some projects, some for university, and some for myself. In this section, I will briefly introduce them to you.

In the following sections, I will call back these projects whenever the songs or the art are related with them.


Soudtracks

I have been composing soundtracks since 2015, inspired by Dark souls music and aesthetic.

Although I have never studied music specifically, I have managed to learn on my own and I have achieved very satisfying results in the recent years.

It's important to mention that not all the music is similar to Dark Souls games, as I have also tried different styles and topics. Let's check them out!
