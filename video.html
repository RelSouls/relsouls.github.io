Racing game final track


This level acts like a "final boss" for the racing game with toy thematic. Unfortunately, the game was never completed and this is only a prototype. However, the manner in which the level and the music complements each other can still be appreciated.

This time, I did design the level and its art style, made the animations for the pieces, composed the music and added the sounds. I did not made the 3D models or program anything besides the events when the animation of the pieces are called.
