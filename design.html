Logo Animation: Red Swamp

Logo Design


Red Swamp is a fictional brand designed for this project. The idea was to have a logo full of different assets so a lot of animation techniques would be implemented in the development of the piece.

Made with Illustrator and After Effects.

Logo Animated


The full animation of Red Swamp
It has three main parts: A preamble, more focused on sensations and textures; the appearance of the logo, bouncy and dynamic; and the staging of the frog, the main symbol of Red Swamp, catching a fly with its tongue.




Animation: Pinball

Pinball: Kimetsu no Yaiba


This pinball was made to put in practice basic principles of animation, such as curves or squash and stretch. The design was heavily inspired by the animated series: Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer).

Click on full screen icon for a better view.

Made with Photoshop and After Effects.




Brand Design: Corteza Hotel

Corteza Hotel: Logo


This brand design is for a fictional luxury hotel located on the outskirts of Madrid. The brief specified that the design should integrate modernity and luxury with the experience of living amidst nature.

The acorn symbol was chosen not only because it is abundant in the Madrid region, but also because of its economic and quality connotations within the context of gastronomy.

Made with Illustrator and Photoshop.

Layout: Arkitekt Magazine

Mockup: Front cover


The brief called for an architecture magazine of the client's choosing that was consistent with the selected style's aesthetic but also aimed at a modern audience.
The iron architecture I chose allowed for a pleasing interplay between the geometry, rectitude, order, and sequentiality of iron, as well as the spaciousness and openness of glass.

Made with InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop.

3D Design: Environmental Still life

Main Camera Render


This still life depicts an enigmatic landscape. The fireflies suggest nighttime, but the color of the sky and the light sow doubt, maintaining a mystical and mysterious tone.

Made with Cinema4D and Redshift.

Main Camera (No trees)


Another version of the image, this time devoid of trees. The mysterious feeling of the previous one shifts towards a more desolate tone, resulting in an image that is not as enigmatic, but certainly more emotional.

Alternate Camera


This camera abandons the oil lamp and the shovel and focuses on the boat. A less mysterious approach, but certainly a bit more tense.




3D Design: Vacuum Perfume

Vacuum


Vacuum is a fictional perfume that seeks to represent the knot of emptiness, an emotional state where emptiness, despite being absence, begins to weigh more than presence itself. The bottle twists around a central hole, as if forming a center of gravity that deforms the glass around it.

Made with Cinema4D and Redshift.
